Profile

I was respectively a computer engineer (by training) for five years and for the past nine years have worked in different universities, primarily in research. I developed various bioinformatics databases and analysis tools (PathEx,gViz... at University of Namur with Prof. Eric Depiereux and Prof. Naji Habra, QuadGT...at University of Montreal with Prof. Daniel Sinnett and Prof. Miklos Csuros). I moved to McGill University in 2013 and there have worked with Prof. Jacek Majewski on various projects (rare diseases[Care For Rare] and cancers[Triple Negative Breast Cancer, Wilms Tumor]...). I have strong interests in scientific computing, genetics and the application of statistical and computational methods in studying human diseases.
